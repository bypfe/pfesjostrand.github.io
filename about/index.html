About

Filip Sjöstrand / 2018-12-02

About Filip Sjöstrand. I am another guy from Sweden. Online I generally go by PFE or some variation of that ; based on my full name Per Filip Emil Sjöstrand. I split my time between Gothenburg, Lund and Stockholm. Contact me on pfesjostrand@gmail.com, for my resume see LinkedIn

My education is in political science (and some economics and statistics). I also have extensive practical experience in politics from volunteering as a local organizer for Socialdemokraterna, Sweden’s social-democratic party. While I am no longer active in any party, politics in different ways have been an interest more or less as far back as I remember and I’d like to keep working with it or related fields in some way.

I like to state opinons: I detest Windows 10. Like beer and Asian food. Love films, here are four good ones: Wild Tales, Taste of Tea, Grave of the fireflies, Tampopo. I think a life well lived should have some struggle in it but also make time for a page-turner on the beach. And while “a man should have a code” a distrust for any complex worldview or ideology is a healthy thing; no matter if we are talking politics, religion or programming.

About website internals. This website was constructed using Hugo and blogdown, an R package built by Yihu Xie, which main benefit is that R code can be written in the contents as in the example below. Pretty awesome if you want to present data analysis.

    c(greeting)
