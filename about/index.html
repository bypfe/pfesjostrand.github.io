About me. Another guy from Sweden. Online I generally go by PFE, pfesjostrand, or some variation of that. My education is in political science (+ some economics and statistics and quite a bit of practical philosophy). I also have extensive practical experience in politics, mainly as a local organizer - on a volunteer basis - for Sweden’s social-democratic party. While I am no longer active in a party, politics in different ways is still a significant interest that I try to find ways to feed.
